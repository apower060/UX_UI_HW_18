About Me

With majors in Sociology and Philosophy from Monash University I am able to plan user tests and research, collate data and present useable findings to contribute to an effective solution.

With a background in mortgages I am highly experienced with user interviews, uncovering unknown needs and finding solutions to problems. I've conducted interviews in person and remotely and can communicate effectively verbally, digitally and in writing

I am UI/UX Designer from Melbourne, Australia working in user interface design and user experience and website development. I enjoy turning complex problems into simple, beautiful and intuitive designs.

My job is to identify problems and provide solutions to facilitate a functional and user friendly product whilst ensuring the solution is aesthetically pleasing. I will add a personal touch to your product and ensure that it is eye-catching and easy to use.

My aim is to project your message and identity in a unique and creative way.
